A little introduction

Hopelessly curious, my goal in life is to be a "Jackie of all trades and maybe master of a few things". I am currently based in Asia and have just embarked on an entrepreneurial journey(or so to say someone who is just really enjoying her unemployment status). In my previous lives, I had worked as a trader and a financial journalist, and now I have a new found love in building websites. I believe in following your bliss in life and contributing something meaningful and positive to the world in the meantime.

What interests me?

Good books,long walks,a little adventure here and there in a foreign land, beauty in nature, art and design, possibilities in technology and our future.

What drives me?

Learning, creativity and personal growth.
